About

This page provides instructions of typical actions performed by users


  • Quick Navigation:

  • Teachers
  • Students and Parents

Teachers

  • Begin by going to the home page


  • Select the Teacher Login button to be taken to a login screen


  • After entering your credentials you will be taken to the teacher dashboard. Here is an example with dummy data

  • Student details can be edited or deleted using the options on the right of each table row

  • The green sidebar allows you to perform different actions on the displayed table, allowing you to:

  • order students by grade, by upcoming due dates, and recent assignments
  • view the student's parents or teachers
  • add new instruments, students, parents, or even a new teacher
  • return to the dashboard


Students and Parents

  • Begin by going to the home page


  • Select the Parent Login button to be taken to a login screen


  • After entering your credentials you will be taken to the student and parent dashboard. Here is an example with dummy data

  • Here, parents can see their children's information including what instrument was checked out and when the return day is
  • Parents also have the option of adding another one of their children
